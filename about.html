About HyScore

HyScore is a performance scoring system designed for hybrid athletes — those who combine both strength and endurance training.

Your HyScore is calculated using your one-rep maxes in the bench press, squat, and deadlift, along with your VO₂ max or endurance benchmark.

The result is a single, percentile-based score out of 100 that reflects your total hybrid fitness — not just your engine or your barbell, but both.

HyScore is built on real strength standards (Mark Rippetoe) and VO₂ max tables (Cooper Institute), and it adjusts for bodyweight and age to keep the comparisons fair.
